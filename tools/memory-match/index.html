Memory match

Eight pairs hidden on a 4×4 grid. Flip two at a time—match them to clear the board. Fewer moves is better.

Moves: 0 Pairs: 0 / 8

Tap a card to begin.

Keyboard: Tab between cards, Enter or Space to flip.

How to play. Tap two face-down cards per turn—if they match, they stay revealed; if not, they flip back. Clear all 8 pairs with as few moves as you can. New game shuffles a fresh board. Keyboard: Tab to move, Enter or Space to flip.
