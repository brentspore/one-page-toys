Blob Choir

Each blob is a tiny synthesizer: detuned oscillators, a quick envelope, and zero musical ambition. Poke them in any order.

Tap a blob to wake the choir.

Audio needs a tap or click first (browser rule). If you hear nothing, try Silence then tap again.

How to use. Tap any blob to trigger its sound (each is a tiny synth). Use Silence to stop everything—browsers need a tap/click before audio plays, so if it’s quiet, try Silence then tap again.
